FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)

What is Nikonekt?

It is an improved vital service delivery platform that aims to increase and speed up efficiency on our power application business process. Nikonekt makes it easy for our customers to make online power application as well as enjoying other self-services at their convenience. Customers can easily get New Power Application service through Nikonekt by using the following ways:

  1. Nikonekt App (from play store & apple store)
  2. Nikonekt web portal
  3. Nikonekt USSD (Dial *152*00#)
  4. Through TANESCO offices (Physical visit)
What are the procedures to be followed by a new applicant to apply for power supply?

The following are the procedures to be followed by a new applicant to make an application for power supply:

  1. Ensure TANESCO distribution infrastructures have reached your area. For a normal Service line, the allowed distance is from 0-120m from the taping pole.
  2. Complete wiring installation at your premise/property by using EWURA registered electrical installation personnel.

    Access the list of eligible electrical installation personnel through nikonekt.tanesco.co.tz or Nikonekt App (which can be freely installed at play store and apple store)

  3. You can conveniently make your power application through the following channels: -
    1. Nikonekt App (from play store & apple store)
    2. Nikonekt web portal
    3. Nikonekt USSD (Dial *152*00#)
    4. Through TANESCO offices (Physical visit)
  4. On successful Submitting an application, an applicant will receive Application Number through direct message in his/her garget.
  5. An applicant will then submit application number and telephone number that was used to make an application to the registered electrical installation personnel for filling wiring details and the installation personnel will submit the same to TANESCO through the Nikonekt Portal upon successful filling wiring details.
  6. TANESCO will then undertake application assessment to crosscheck correctness of data submitted by applicant and electrical installation personnel. vii. Application with incorrect data/details will be returned to the contractor or applicant for correction and resubmission.
  7. Application with correct data will be processed for surveying.
  8. After survey, an applicant will receive control number through direct message. Applicant may at any time download quotation and government bill by selecting Check Status through the Nikonekt: nikonekt.tanesco.co.tz
  9. An applicant will then make payment through bank, Mobile money, Simbanking or any other convenient payment method of his/her choice.
  10. TANESCO will then connect power to the applicant as stipulated on the Customer Service Charter.

I sometime get errors at the moment I top up my meter, What are the source of those errors and what can I do to resolve those type of errors.

There are sometime errors that can occur during meter top up or meter operation. We recommend that if you’re not conversant with the displayed error, call TANESCO at 0748550000 for immediate assistance. Otherwise,

  1. Crosscheck whether tokens your toping up belongs to the respective meter, Tokens purchased for another meter cannot be processed into a different meter.
  2. Check whether there is electricity at your area or your Circuit breaker is switched on or off. Make sure it is switched on.
  3. Check the message that display in your remote, see sampled token reject alerts and action required to resolve the error here below: -
    1. If it Display error 77, for meters starting with 24... press 0Meternumber0meternumber then ok to clear the error
    2. If it Display connecting, for meter starting with 37..., Press 59698686 Meter Number then Ok,
    3. If it Display connecting for meter staring with 54 press meter Number, then okay.

    In case of failure to clear the error after following the above procedure call our call Centre for an assistance as started above.

I think my meter is running faster than normal. Can I request for meter accuracy check from TANESCO?

YES; But Customer may consider the following before making an application for meter accuracy check: -

  1. Crosscheck the load of electricity equipment your using
  2. Crosscheck the quality of wiring installation at your premise by using qualified and approved electrical installation personnel.
  3. Crosscheck the quality of electricity equipment used on undertaking wiring at the respective building
  4. Crosscheck the absoluteness of the wiring at the respective building
  5. Undertaking self-test by switching off all electrical appliances for sometimes and check whether token will be consumed, If there is a decrease in token balance report to TANESCO.
  6. Use eligible electrical installation personnel to undertake wiring inspection at the building to reduce energy losses. It is recommended for residential houses to be inspected after every five years.

After considering all the above issues and yet the customer is not satisfied with energy consumption pattern at his or her premise a customer may file a meter accuracy check to test for crosschecking.

I’m a tenant and I used my money to connect with electricity the house I rented. Now I want to vacate the rented house to my new house. Can I shift the meter which I incurred cost from TANESCO as the landlord is not ready to refund me back?
  1. NO. Energy Meter is an equipment which is used to measure customer electricity consumption.
  2. The customer does not pay for the energy meter. The meter becomes TANESCO’s property all the time. Customer pays only for other installation charges and a little amount as meter deposit.
  3. Thus why when energy meter become defective for any reason which is not caused by a customer, TANESCO will replace it at her cost as it is her equipment.
  4. TANESCO customer is responsible for equipment which are beyond the energy meter if found malfunctioning, that is why even some electrical installation personnel is not allowed to service or touch the energy meter or break the seals.
  5. The moment an energy meter is installed, it cannot be shifted from one point to another unless it is within one plot and it is done by TANESCO upon customer’s special request.
  6. If you spot any anomalies at our energy meter or any equipment from the meter to the pole, please inform TANESCO immediately and we will attend the problem at no cost.
